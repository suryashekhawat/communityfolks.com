#CommaConf - Community Matters Mini Conference
We bring to you India's First Ever Community(read: People) Conference powered by Facebook CLC Bangalore. It is planned to be less formal and more of a get-together. We want your energy at the highest levels, as we want discussions to flow. Just sitting and listening will not be allowed! We want a casual kind of vibe, as we plan to host a variety of panel discussions, AMAs, talks, and unconference. Your input and involvement are of utmost importance, right from the start. All of us community managers always work for the people, sometimes it is good when things happen for us.
